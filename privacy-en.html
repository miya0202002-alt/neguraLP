NEGURA PRIVACY POLICY

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 9 February 2026
Last updated: 28 February 2026

This website (hereinafter, "the Site") is operated by negura (hereinafter, "the Facility"). The Facility respects the privacy of all visitors and strives to appropriately protect personal information and related information. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled on the Site.

1. Information collected on this Site

The Site does not provide contact forms or other fields for actively entering your name, email address, or similar personal details. Merely browsing the Site does not cause the Facility to actively obtain information that directly identifies you.

However, in the course of operating the Site, the following types of information may be collected automatically by servers or external services:

  • General access logs such as access date and time, IP address, browser and device type
  • Identifiers such as cookies and information on usage, including pages viewed and time spent
  • Access information collected by external services (microCMS, Google Analytics, Airbnb, Instagram, Google Maps, etc.)

2. Purposes of use

Information obtained via the Site is mainly used for the following purposes:

  • To analyze and improve the stability and quality of how the Site is displayed and operates
  • To prevent and respond to unauthorized access, security incidents, and similar issues
  • To use as reference data when providing information and announcements about the Facility
  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations and for other legitimate purposes incidental to these

3. External services, third-party provision, and joint use

The Site uses the following external services for purposes such as distributing information and displaying maps:

  • microCMS (headless CMS used to publish news content)
  • Services provided by Google LLC (Google Analytics, Google Maps, etc.)
  • Airbnb (links and widgets for accommodation reservations)
  • Meta Platforms, Inc. (Instagram embeds and links)

Information obtained and processed through these external services is managed in accordance with each service provider's privacy policy. Except where required by law, the Facility does not sell or lend information collected via the Site to third parties without your consent.

Please note that the servers used by these services, including microCMS and Google, may be located outside your country or region. As a result, information may be provided to business operators based overseas. In such cases, the Facility will obtain your consent as required under applicable laws before providing information, and you may withdraw your consent (opt-out) at any time by changing settings or following the procedures provided by each service.

4. Cookies and access analytics tools

The Site currently uses Google Analytics, an access analytics service provided by Google LLC, in order to understand usage and improve content. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect and record how the Site is used, and the resulting data is stored and processed by Google.

The collected information may include IP addresses, pages viewed, length of stay, and information about the browsing environment. This information is used for statistical analysis and does not directly identify specific individuals.

You can refuse cookies or delete previously stored cookies via your browser settings. Please be aware that in such cases some features of the Site may not function correctly. For details about how Google Analytics collects and uses data, and how to opt out, please refer to the information provided directly by Google.

5. Security measures

In operating the Site, the Facility takes appropriate technical and organizational security measures to help protect access logs and other information from unauthorized access, loss, alteration, or leakage.

6. Compliance with laws and revisions to this Policy

The Facility complies with applicable personal data protection laws, regulations, and guidelines. The contents of this Privacy Policy are reviewed as necessary and may be revised. When important changes are made, they will be announced on the Site and the latest version will be published.
