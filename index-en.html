A quiet hideaway
with a bird host.

Once a traditional doll shop, this house has been reborn as a small inn nestled in the town. “Tonight's nest is here.” We welcome those who come to Setouchi to enjoy a slow, temporary way of living. The little bird curled up in its nest is, in fact, the host of this inn. So that humans, animals, and all living things can truly rest, we quietly keep evolving everyone's “negura” day by day.

About negura

A small bird quietly watches over the nest. As the “host” of this inn, it stands here every day so that every traveler who visits can rest their wings in comfort.

Phase 01

Preparation
